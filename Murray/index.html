Murray Kentucky

"Rand McNally and USA Today awarded the "2012 Friendliest Small Town in America" to Murray, Kentucky. The award came as a result of the "Best of the Road" competition, where a team of two travelers went cross country, spending three days in each finalist city. Although Murray was the first stop on the month-long trip, the community spirit stuck with the visitors, and they selected the town as the ultimate winner."
